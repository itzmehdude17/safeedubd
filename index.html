About Us

What is Safe Education Consultancy?

Safe Education Consultancy is working to help Bangladeshi students, in fulfilling their dreams of pursuing abroad education. Our team is made up full of energetic and young yet experienced people who are currently studying or have completed their higher education in countries like the USA, Canada, the UK, Australia and New Zealand.

We are grounded on the motto of helping you fulfill your dreams of obtaining abroad education, with accurate information and practical guidance.
